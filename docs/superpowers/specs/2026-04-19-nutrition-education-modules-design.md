# Nutrition Education Modules — Design Spec
**Date:** 2026-04-19  
**Project:** Number One HSP Members Area  
**Scope:** Beginner nutrition education series — content design, slide themes, scripts

---

## Overview

A 7-module beginner nutrition education series for the Number One HSP members area at `members.numberonehsp.com/education`. Content covers calorie tracking, MyFitnessPal, macronutrients, intermittent fasting, nutrition myths, recovery & DOMS, and supplements.

The series fits into the existing education hub infrastructure (pathways, sequential modules, video embeds, quizzes, PDF downloads — all already built).

---

Modules are distributed across three pathways in the existing system:

| # | Module | Format | Approx. length |

## Format Definitions

**A-format:** Single video + slide deck. No quiz. No downloadable notes.  
**D-format:** Multiple lessons, each with video + slides. Quiz (multiple choice + true/false) after key lessons. Each D-format module has its own 1-page summary sheet PDF (3 sheets total: macro reference card, myth vs. fact card, supplement decision tree).

### Slide Types

**Title slide:** Dark background with geometric polygon texture overlay (matches logo aesthetic). Module number, module title in white bold caps, horizontal teal rule, pathway/phase label below.

**Content slide:** Clean dark background. Teal section label top-left. White bold headline. Teal bullet points with pale teal body text. Optional graphic/icon panel on right.

**Key takeaway slide:** Dark background with subtle polygon texture + teal border. Teal "Key Takeaway" label. White bold quote — the one sentence members should remember.

### Delivery Format
- **Slides:** Google Slides, 16:9 widescreen
- **Scripts:** Paired Google Doc, one doc per module
- **Summary sheets:** Google Docs → exported as PDF, uploaded via `pdf_url` field in Supabase

## Script Structure

Every video (A-format and each D-format lesson) follows this 5-part structure:

| Section | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| ① Hook | 15–20 sec | Opens on a pain point or bold statement that earns attention |
| ② Signpost | 20–30 sec | Tells members what they'll know by the end |
| ③ Content | Bulk of video | 45–90 sec per slide. Jargon defined immediately when introduced. Max 3 key points per slide. Tone: direct, evidence-based, no hype, always explain terminology |
| ④ Key takeaway | 20–30 sec | One memorable sentence that captures the whole lesson |
| ⑤ Action step + next | 20 sec | One practical thing to do today. Tease of next module/lesson |

D-format lessons add a **quiz prompt** after the action step: *"Before you move on, hit the quiz below — it takes about two minutes and really cements what we've covered."*

### Tone
Direct and evidence-based but approachable — confident and honest, not preachy or salesy. Any jargon introduced is defined immediately in plain language within the script.

### Presenter
Scripts written in a neutral, adaptable voice. Any coach can record any module in their own style.

---

## Quiz Format

Applies to D-format modules only. Mixed multiple choice (4 options, 1 correct) and true/false. 3–5 questions per lesson. Delivered using the existing quiz infrastructure in `SEED_QUIZ` / Supabase `education_quizzes` table.
